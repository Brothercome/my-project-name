Motion Designer

As a Motion Designer at DOR, you will design and create templates, effects, and transitions specialized for game videos so that gamers can more easily and more impressively turn their gameplay into content. You will build a system that enables anyone to create short, impactful videos without advanced editing skills.

Apply Now Coffee Chat
Key Responsibilities
  • Plan and produce short-form video templates specialized for game videos.
  • Design effects, transitions, captions, and visual elements using After Effects and Premiere Pro.
  • Improve template quality and usability so users can create better results more easily.
  • Analyze which templates and effects generate higher engagement based on data and feedback, and iterate improvements.
  • Collaborate with PM, designers, engineers, and operations to ensure templates connect to the actual product experience.
  • Create DOR's unique video language by reflecting game-specific aesthetics, user preferences, and short-form trends.
Key Challenges in This Role

We're looking for someone who designs templates and effects that many users can use, rather than someone who just makes great videos.

  • Enabling gamers to create impressive videos without advanced editing skills
  • Capturing different game aesthetics and scenes effectively in short-form video language
  • Creating output that people actually want to share, not just flashy effects
  • Building structures where templates are repeatedly used, not just one-off pretty designs
  • Connecting short-form trends into the product's creation experience
Products You'll Build
Automatic Gameplay Recording (Record)
  • Enable users to automatically capture moments they don't want to miss.
  • Make event-based automatic recording more accurate and seamless.
  • The goal is to create an experience where "it just records well without any setup."
Game Video Creation (Create)
  • Turn auto-generated clips into short, watchable videos.
  • Design the product so that the output is satisfying even without manual editing.
  • Create an experience where content is completed without "tedious and difficult editing."
Game Video Editor (Edit)
  • Design effects and UX specialized for game video editing.
  • Build a product that isn't as complex as professional tools but maintains high output satisfaction.
  • Aim for a creation experience where anyone can quickly produce video content.
Sharing Experience (Share)
  • Make it easy for users to share created videos with friends and communities.
  • Design the experience so it doesn't end at video creation but leads to actual sharing and viewing.
How We Work
  • DOR builds products that customers want, not products the CEO wants.
  • We form hypotheses, build, measure, learn, and repeat in short 1-2 week iterations.
  • We build products with autonomy and accountability, based on data and user feedback.
  • Designers don't just create deliverables -- they define problems and create results.
  • We build fast, experiment fast, and adjust direction fast.
  • We obsessively focus on creating content experiences that make users want to share more easily.

Qualifications
  • Strong understanding of video editing and motion design using After Effects, Premiere Pro, etc.
  • Understanding of short-form video language with hands-on creation experience
  • Ability to design reusable video elements such as templates, transitions, and effects
  • Ability to learn fast and execute fast
  • Strong understanding of gaming, video, and community spaces
  • Desire to create videos that get shared and consumed more, not just pretty videos
Preferred Qualifications
  • Experience creating or managing content on TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, etc.
  • Experience creating short-form video templates
  • Experience with VFX, captions, transitions, or template system design
  • Experience managing social media channels and analyzing performance
  • Experience working at a startup or gaming-related company
  • English reading or communication ability
  • Experience creating measurable content performance (subscribers, views, shares, etc.)
  • A gamer or someone with deep understanding of gameplay scenes
You'd Be a Great Fit If You Are...
  • Someone more interested in creating templates many users can use than a single great video
  • Someone who wants to go beyond following short-form trends to connecting them into the product experience
  • Someone who values shareable, reaction-generating output over pretty visuals
  • Someone who iterates based on data and feedback, not just instinct
  • Someone who finds joy in making game scenes look more impressive and communicative
  • Someone who wants to create the video effects and language used by gamers worldwide
What to Highlight in Your Resume
  • Show us what videos, templates, and effects you've created so far.
  • Share not just the output but why you made it that way and what responses or results it generated.
  • If you have quantitative results such as views, likes, shares, saves, or subscriber growth, please include them.
  • If you've directly managed a short-form video channel or achieved measurable results, please share.
  • Rather than listing work history and credentials, we'd love to see your motivation, thought process, actions, learnings, and outcomes.
