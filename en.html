Why can a global #1 emerge from Korea?

Success in social media requires three things: a large market, strong network effects, and influencers.
Korea is the only country that has all three.

Accessible Large Market
NEXON, KRAFTON, NC, Netmarble. All top global game companies are based in Korea, one of the largest gaming markets in the world.
Strong Network Effects
High gaming adoption among teens and twenties, a dense market, and rapid trend diffusion. Strong network effects are already in place.
Global Influencers
Faker, T1, Gen.G. The world's best pro gamers and esports teams are in Korea. It's a market where the top gamers exist.
