CEO Staff Intern

Work directly under the CEO to think through and execute strategic challenges across the company. This is a position where you can experience the decision-making process of an early-stage startup from the closest seat. We're looking for someone who wants to learn fast, execute fast, and experience firsthand how a startup operates.

Apply Now Coffee Chat

Key Responsibilities

The CEO Staff Intern works directly under the CEO to think through and execute strategic challenges across the company.

This position is different from typical internships that support a specific team's work. You will directly tackle the challenges the CEO considers most important and experience the entire startup decision-making process.

What the CEO Staff Does

  • Support research and analysis needed for CEO decision-making.
  • Prepare and manage meetings with strategic partners, investors, and external stakeholders.
  • Manage and coordinate major internal project progress.
  • Support executive scheduling and communications.
  • Research and report on global gaming and tech trends.
  • Support team culture events and hiring process operations.

What Matters Most in This Role

We're not looking for a simple supporter, but someone who thinks alongside the CEO and executes directly.

  • Ability to quickly organize complex information and communicate key points
  • An attitude of identifying tasks proactively and moving first rather than waiting for instructions
  • Ability to communicate flexibly with various departments, external partners, and investors
  • Ability to adapt to rapid startup changes and quickly reprioritize

What You'll Experience

Strategy Development & Execution

  • See firsthand how the CEO sets priorities during the early stages of a startup.
  • Experience various decision-making processes up close, including fundraising, partnerships, hiring, and product direction.

Global Gaming & Tech Industry

  • Gain deep understanding of global gaming, social media, and creator economy trends.
  • Build experience communicating with global networks including San Francisco.

Rapid Growth Environment

  • Go beyond simple work experience to develop a perspective that understands the entire company's flow.
  • If performance is strong, there is an opportunity for full-time conversion after the internship.

How We Work

  • DOR moves based on data and user feedback, not the CEO's preferred direction.
  • We quickly form hypotheses, validate fast, and adjust direction fast.
  • We value the ability and attitude to actually solve problems over education or credentials.
  • CEO Staff is not simply a role that assists the CEO -- it's a role that helps the entire team move better.

Qualifications

  • No education requirements (currently enrolled students welcome)
  • Strong interest in startups and the tech industry
  • Fast learning ability and proactive work attitude
  • Basic communication ability in Korean and English

Preferred Qualifications

  • Deep understanding of gaming, social media, and global tech
  • Previous startup internship or team project experience
  • Basic data analysis skills (Excel, SQL, etc.)
  • Experience with research, report writing, or presentations
  • Interest in full-time conversion

You'd Be a Great Fit If You Are...

  • Someone who wants to see and learn firsthand how a startup operates
  • Someone who wants to explore diverse experiences to find their own direction
  • Someone who identifies tasks and moves first rather than waiting for instructions
  • Someone who excels at quickly organizing complex information and communicating it
  • Someone who wants a rapid growth environment working directly with the CEO

What to Highlight in Your Resume

  • If you've proactively led and solved problems or tasks on your own, please share.
  • If you have experience with research, analysis, organization, or presentations, please describe in detail.
  • Tell us about the motivation or experience that sparked your interest in startups or the tech industry.
  • Rather than listing education and credentials, we'd love to see your motivation, thought process, and growth journey.
